Preparing Our Students for BECE & SHS

Comprehensive Guidance for Future Success

Academic Excellence & BECE Preparation

Our Junior High School curriculum is designed to rigorously prepare students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE). We provide a strong foundation in core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies to ensure students are well-equipped for the exam and further studies in Senior High School (SHS).

  • Regular mock exams and assessments to build confidence and mastery of topics.
  • Personalized tutoring for students who need additional support.
  • Comprehensive coverage of BECE syllabus.
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skill development.

Character Building & Life Skills

At Maria Montessori, we emphasize more than just academic success. We prepare our students for life beyond school with a focus on character development and essential life skills.

  • Leadership training and public speaking opportunities.
  • Time management and study skills workshops.
  • Ethics, teamwork, and collaboration through projects and group activities.
  • Guidance on choosing the right Senior High School and career pathways.
