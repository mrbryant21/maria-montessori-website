"to will and act for thyself"

As many of you know, Maria Montessori School has very humble beginnings. It is through hard work and dedication that we are now counted among the best in basic school education in the metropolis.

Our Story

Founded in 1989 in Kumasi by Mrs. Grace Boaten, the school started in a family home with just ten children. Quickly, it grew into a nurturing nursery, becoming known in the community as a great place for toddlers.

Our Growth

Responding to the need for quality education, the school expanded to include a primary school by 1992, operating initially from a family residence. Despite challenges, including a severe rainstorm in 1993, Maria Montessori School moved to a new, spacious site in Odeneho Kwadaso, accommodating a growing number of students.

Our Success

Today, Maria Montessori School is a respected institution known for high academic standards. Our students excel academically, morally, and socially, laying strong foundations for future success.

With over 35 years of experience, we offer a balanced co-educational environment, focusing on individual potential and personal growth.

We provide counseling support, especially for our junior secondary students, ensuring they are well-prepared for the future.

Policies

ADMISSION POLICY

Maria Montessori School welcomes students committed to personal and academic growth in a supportive, inclusive environment. The school values discipline, independence, and a positive attitude toward learning. Admission may involve an assessment to ensure students are placed appropriately for their educational development.

QUALITY EDUCATION

The school offers the Ghana Education Service (G.E.S) curriculum in a broad and challenging manner, providing opportunities for children to develop their talents. The quality of teaching is high, with supportive teachers and good relations between students, parents, and teachers, creating an effective learning environment.

CODE OF CONDUCT

All members of the school community are expected to show respect and care for each other. This includes respecting the individuality of students, teachers, and staff and promoting a friendly and caring atmosphere.

MISSION, VISION, AND CORE VALUES

MISSION

To cultivate a culture of caring and academic excellence within a nurturing environment, promoting individual growth, self-esteem, and responsibility among students while ensuring they are prepared for their highest potential in learning and examinations.

VISION

To foster happy, confident learners who thrive in an engaging educational atmosphere, encouraging independence, self-motivation, and the development of each child's unique talents while offering a broad and challenging curriculum.

CORE VALUES

  • Individual Care
  • Academic Excellence
  • Community and Responsibility
  • Independence and Confidence
  • Challenging Curriculum
