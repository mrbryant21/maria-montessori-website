Lower Primary: A World of Discovery

Our Lower Primary focuses on hands-on learning and exploration in literacy, numeracy, and environmental studies. Pupils build their knowledge while fostering creativity and social skills.

Age
6 - 8 Years
Activity
Literacy (Reading & Writing)
Numeracy (Basic Arithmetic)
Art & Craft

Upper Primary: Empowering Future Leaders

The Upper Primary program builds on foundational skills with a focus on critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership. Pupils engage in deeper subject knowledge and project-based learning.

Age
9 - 11 Years
Activity
Advanced Literacy (Grammar & Composition)
Mathematics (Problem Solving)
Science Projects
History & Geography
Leadership Training
