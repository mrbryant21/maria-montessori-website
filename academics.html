Primary Education

Our Lower Primary program focuses on developing core skills in reading, writing, and mathematics. We encourage children to think critically, work collaboratively, and develop a love for learning.

In Upper Primary, we introduce more complex concepts and subjects, fostering creativity and a deeper understanding of the world.

Learn More

Junior High School

In Junior High School, students build on their primary education, exploring core subjects while adjusting to a more structured academic environment.

The final year focuses on intensive preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), consolidating their knowledge across all subjects.
