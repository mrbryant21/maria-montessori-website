Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to learn more about our programs and to schedule a visit.

Step 2: Application

Fill out the application form available on our website and submit the required documents.

Step 3: Interview

Attend an interview with our admissions team to discuss your goals and expectations.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Transcripts from previous school(s)
  • Recommendation letter from current school
  • Medical records
